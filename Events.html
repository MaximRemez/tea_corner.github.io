About events

Events are held in our tea house. Some of them are weekly, while others are timed to coincide with major holidays. During festive events, you can get into the appropriate atmosphere. Also, a unique dish or drink is served for any event.
*During holiday events, weekly events do not work.
**You cannot order delivery of meals timed to events.
