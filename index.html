About us

Cafe "TeaCorner" was founded in 1993. In those years, it was a small cafe that sold only tea. And only at the beginning of 2000, we expanded the assortment and changed the style. It successfully combines the zeitgeist and modern trends.

What is our atmosphere

Interier

Our cafe has a sophisticated style. It combines Provence and modernity. "TeaCorner" is designed for small groups of people. We have a calm atmosphere that helps fruitful work and pleasant communication. We also have free high-speed Wi-Fi, for comfortable work on a laptop with a cup of tea.
